Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025  ·  Hangzhou ChiXing Game Technology Co., Ltd.

We believe privacy is a right. This policy explains clearly and honestly what data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control it.

1. Who We Are

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile games and services published by Hangzhou ChiXing Game Technology Co., Ltd. ("ChiXing Games", "we", "our", or "us"), headquartered in Hangzhou, China. You can reach us at admin@chixinggame.com.

2. Information We Collect

When you play our games, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS, with your permission), and language settings.
  • Gameplay Data: Game progress, scores, session duration, and in-game events. This helps us improve game balance and your experience.
  • Advertising Data: If you have granted permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt, advertising identifiers may be used to show you personalized ads.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous technical data to help us identify and fix bugs.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve our games
  • To display advertisements (including rewarded and banner ads via Google AdMob)
  • To analyze gameplay and fix technical issues
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

Our games use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads based on your interests. On iOS, we will ask for your permission via the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before accessing your advertising identifier (IDFA).

If you decline tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized. You can change your preference at any time in your device's Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share limited data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate our games (e.g., analytics, advertising networks), under strict data processing agreements.
  • Law enforcement if required by applicable law.

6. Children's Privacy

Our games are rated 4+ and are designed to be appropriate for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately at admin@chixinggame.com and we will delete it promptly.

7. Data Retention

We retain gameplay and analytics data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations, typically no longer than 24 months. Advertising identifiers are processed according to Google AdMob's data retention policies.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to access the data we hold about you
  • Right to request deletion of your data
  • Right to opt out of personalized advertising (via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking)
  • If you are in the EU/EEA: rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to object
  • If you are in California: rights under CCPA including the right to know and the right to delete

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@chixinggame.com.

9. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use the latest version of our apps to benefit from the most recent security updates.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of our games after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We recommend checking this page periodically.
